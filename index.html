IMPORTANT NEWS FOR UPCOMING SEASON 2019/20

To all Iveagh Youth branch members and other One United members wishing to become members of our branch.

News as to how the branch will be run this season is as follows:

The first time the branch will be taking names and deposits for all games up the end of the season. This will also apply to people who wish to make their own travel arrangements and apply for ticket only. Priority for those games most in demand will be decided by the support shown to Man United and the branch

For the Man Utd v Liverpool game a ballot is being run and funds raised will be used to provide family priced trips where a lower price for children will be available. Names to contact for booking trips, ticket only and books of ballots will be shown at the end of the post.

We are in the process of upgrading the rooms in the hotels we use to executive rooms and we have acquired some grade 1 season tickets for the new season. We are also in the process of adding a new 4-star hotel to our accommodation list

It is our intention, if possible, to retain the very inexpensive pricing structure that has been available to our members for the past number of years, and of course we will remain 1 of the few if not only branches that do not enforce any form of dues.

Free entry to various competitions to win Manchester United memorabilia and free tickets for games will be available for all those who share and promote all branch news on their social media platforms. Further news to follow!

WELCOME TO OUR NEW WEBSITE

The start of the new season is upon us, and with the new season comes our new website which is a work in progress.

The purpose of our new website is as follows. For members existing and new to:

  1. Find important trip and ticket information in a timely, accessible manner.
  2. Stay up to date with official club news, competitions and special offers
  3. Find out more about how to join the club

We have also launched official Facebook, Instagram and Twitter channels to help you to stay up to date with club news on the platform of your choice. Help spread the word by giving us a like and a follow.

We're very excited about the upcoming season and look forward to seeing you all at future games.
